About WoodAllGood

Sales 6,724
On Etsy since 2016

Hello and welcome!

What started out as a passionate hobby soon turned into something more. In early 2015 I decided to take up wood working as a hobby, with nothing but my Grandfathers rusty old hand tools and a good pair of hands.

My hobby soon turned into an obsession and I found myself spending every bit of my spare time in my workshop, often very late into the night. Around this time I also stumbled upon resin by chance. I fell in love with the material right away and imagined all the things I could do with it. Having worked with wood I naturally started combining both materials together, with amazingly unique results. I especially loved how my resin and wood jewelry turned out, so I started offering it as gifts for my close friends.

As my skill grew so did my confidence. With encouragement from my friends and family, seeing how much they loved my work, I decided to open an Etsy shop... and I was so thrilled to find out that other people also loved my stuff!

Creating wood and resin jewelry and other designs is still my love and passion today. My jewelry is designed to be simple yet complex, combining two opposite materials into one: resin and wood. I only use non-toxic jewelry grade resin and sustainable sourced wood. Some designs also include other materials such as natural and man-made stones, precious and semi-precious metals, as well as natural materials such as dried flowers, moss and lichen.

I love exploring new ideas and I am always open to suggestions and custom requests. Don't hesitate to send me a message if you have any questions, I'll be happy to help!

Thank you so much for making this possible by supporting our little corner of the internet!
- Miha

- DELIVERY DATE: When your package arrives to you. Estimated delivery time = processing time + shipping time.

PRODUCT CARE

Resin/wood items:

- Use a soft cloth

- Avoid submerging your piece in water. Wipe dry after your piece has been exposed to water

- Avoid exposure to extreme heat and avoid leaving your piece sitting in the sun for longer periods of time.

- Do not use chemical cleaners, including household cleaners, nail polish remover and other similar products.

- Do not use abrasive cleaners and avoid abrasive surfaces.


Our products made from solid wood only also require a reapplication of wood treating oils every couple of years, or once the item shows signs of aging.

Please contact me if you need any assistance.
